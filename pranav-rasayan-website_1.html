About The Firm

Rooted in the field, not just the lab.

Pranav Rasayan Pvt. Ltd. began with a simple belief — that a farmer's trust is earned one good harvest at a time. What started as a small formulation unit has grown into a firm that dealers and farmers across the region rely on for honest, effective crop protection.

Every product that leaves our facility is tested against real field conditions first, not just lab benchmarks — because a pesticide that doesn't survive monsoon humidity or a sudden pest surge isn't solving anything for the farmer holding it.
